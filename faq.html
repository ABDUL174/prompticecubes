Frequently Asked Questions

Some of the queries you want to know about us.

What types of ice products do you offer?

We provide high-quality ice cubes, ice blocks, ice slabs, and ice boxes suitable for personal, event, and commercial use.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We provide fast and reliable delivery across Kakkanad, Infopark, Ernakulam, and Kochi to ensure you get your ice when you need it.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by calling us, messaging us. We ensure a quick response and hassle-free ordering process.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity depends on the type of ice product you need. Contact us for details, and we’ll guide you based on your requirements.

How do you ensure the quality of your ice?

We use advanced purification and freezing techniques to produce clean, crystal-clear, and long-lasting ice that meets hygiene and safety standards.

Do you offer bulk orders for businesses and events?

Absolutely! We cater to restaurants, bars, hotels, events, and commercial businesses with bulk supply options at competitive rates.
